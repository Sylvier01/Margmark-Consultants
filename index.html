Our Services

What we do:

We offer a comprehensive range of consultancy and support services tailored to the needs of organizations in Kenya:

Fumigation and Decontamination

We provide professional fumigation and decontamination services to control pests, eliminate harmful microorganisms, and maintain hygienic environments in workplaces, institutions, and facilities.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

Our experts conduct Environmental Impact Assessments to help organizations identify, evaluate, and manage environmental risks in compliance with national regulations and best practices.

Occupational Safety and Health (OSH)

We support organizations in creating safe and healthy workplaces through risk assessments, safety audits, policy development, and compliance with occupational safety and health standards.

HACCP Audits

We carry out Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP) audits to help food-related businesses ensure food safety, improve quality control, and meet regulatory requirements.

First Aid Services

We provide first aid training and support services to equip staff with essential lifesaving skills and ensure workplace readiness in case of emergencies.

Capacity Building

Through training, workshops, and advisory services, we strengthen organizational capacity by enhancing staff knowledge, skills, and compliance awareness.

About Us

Margmark Consultants is a professional consultancy and services firm based in Kenya, established to support organizations in meeting operational, safety, health, and environmental standards. Our team consists of experienced and competent professionals with a strong understanding of regulatory requirements and industry best practices.
